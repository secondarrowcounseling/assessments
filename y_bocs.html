Second Arrow Counseling Yale-Brown Obsessive-Compulsive Scale (Y-BOCS) Replica

1. Patient Information & Instructions

OBSESSIONS are unwelcomed and distressing ideas, thoughts, or impulses that repeatedly enter your mind[cite: 2]. They may seem to occur against your will[cite: 2]. Examples include recurrent impulses to do harm or severe fear of contamination[cite: 2].

COMPULSIONS are behaviors or acts that you feel driven to perform although you may recognize them as senseless or excessive[cite: 2]. Examples include checking door locks repeatedly, handwashing, or mental rituals like silent repeating phrases[cite: 2].
